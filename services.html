Solar Panels

Latent Technology provides the best in class service for installation, maintainance and quoting for solar panels for residential and commercial buildings. We have qualified technicians to access the best location for solar panels and install them. Our technicians are instructed to use only high efficiency Mono-crystalline solar panels. Our Installation services has following features:

  • Determine the size of solar system, based on current usage demands
  • Assisting you in submitting applications for solar rebates, and necessary permits
  • Full report of type of components to be installed
  • Durable installation from experienced technicians
  • Checking for any leaks in the roof or moisture build up

Before Installation

As professional and experienced installers we value our customer and provide accurate Solar PV. We provide you the best in class before installation service. Our service features for before installations are:

  • Assess the best location for solar panels on your roof or property
  • Determine the size of solar system, based on current usage demands
  • Determine whether it is worth replacing your roof, or reinforcing it
  • Determine if your electrical service panel is large enough for the additional solar load
  • Full report of type of components to be installed
  • Overview of available subsidies and financing options

After Installation

We will never leave you in hasle after installing the panels. We provide maintaince and after installation services without charging you a much for service.

  • For the first year we will check for any leaks in the roof or moisture build up
  • Empowering you by best apps for Monitoring performance of your system
  • We will assist in submitting applications for solar rebates, and necessary permits
  • We will also work with your electricity company to arrange connections to the grid
